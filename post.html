What We Aim to Achieve

EquiWork is designed to bridge the accessibility gap in workplaces across Wales. Our goal is to create a tool that empowers disabled employees, visitors, and clients by providing real-time information and support to navigate physical and digital barriers.

We envision a future where every workplace is inclusive, ensuring that everyone, regardless of ability, can thrive independently and confidently.

The app will be based in Wales, tested and launched in Cardiff. With the help of the wonderful welsh organisations and advocacy groups, our reach will only continue to grow, helping people from all across the world.

Creating workplaces where everyone feels welcome and supported.

Key Features of EquiWork

Our app is packed with features designed to address the unique challenges faced by disabled individuals in the workplace:

  • Voice-Activated Navigation: Helps users navigate workplaces independently.
  • Accessibility Ratings: Provides real-time ratings of workplace accessibility features.
  • Emergency Assistance: Offers tailored support during urgent situations.
  • Communication Tools: Includes sign language interpretation and alternative text formats.
  • Feedback Mechanism: Allows users to report issues and suggest improvements.

The app will address key challenges faced by disabled employees including lack of real-time accessibility information which hinders independent navigation. It will improve communication support such as sign language interpretation and alternative text formats for those with hearing or visual impairments. It will also enhance emergency procedures which often disregard the needs of disabled individuals to create a more inclusive workplace.

Our app provides real-time information on accessibility features like ramps, lifts, and accessible toilets, digital tools such as screen readers and voice-activated navigation, and enhanced emergency procedures tailored to individual needs.

EquiWork is build for disabled employees who face workplace challenges, who will benefit most, but visitors with disabilities, clients and even contractors will also benefit from the app.
