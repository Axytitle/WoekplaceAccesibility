About Us

Empowering workplaces through accessibility and inclusion

EquiWork is a groundbreaking initiative designed to transform workplaces into inclusive environments for everyone. Our mission is to address the challenges faced by disabled employees, visitors, and clients by providing real-time accessibility solutions that empower independence and promote inclusivity.

We believe that accessibility is not just a legal requirement but a moral imperative. By leveraging technology, we aim to bridge the gap between physical and digital barriers, ensuring that every workplace in Wales and beyond, becomes a space where everyone can thrive.

Our team is made up of passionate individuals, including software developers, accessibility specialists, and advocates for disability rights. Together, we are committed to creating a tool that not only meets the needs of today but also evolves to address the challenges of tomorrow.
